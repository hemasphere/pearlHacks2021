What do we do?

Health geeks is a web app that downloads your health data and sends it to your doctor. This allows you doctor to keep track of your data and is alerted in case of some inconsistency in your records.

Next

Here's how to works

This web app downloads your data from either Apple Health or Samsung Health. It parses through the data it receives. It is now in a legible format where you can see your respiratory intake, blood pressure, steps and active calories. This information is then sent to your doctor who is able to track the data.
